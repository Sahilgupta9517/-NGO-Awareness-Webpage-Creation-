Our Identity

Among Friends: Our Story

InAmigos Foundation is a youth-led movement redefining social impact for the digital age. We've transformed the traditional NGO model into a decentralized, community-driven network where change happens through friendship and collective action. We believe that when young minds unite, impact isn't just a goal—it's a movement.
